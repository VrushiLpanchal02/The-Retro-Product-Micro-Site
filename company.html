About Regency Electronics

Regency Electronics is an Indianapolis-based company bringing transistor technology into everyday radio sets for families across America.

Our Model TR-1 pocket radio proves that powerful reception and stylish design can fit in the palm of your hand.

Our Story

Founded by radio engineers with a passion for innovation, Regency started by building television and radio accessories before turning to all-transistor radios.

The TR-1 project focused on replacing bulky vacuum tubes with compact germanium transistors to create a truly portable receiver.

Our Mission

  • Make radio listening possible anywhere, from city streets to countryside picnics.
  • Combine reliable performance with bold mid-century styling.
  • Lead the way in commercial transistor applications.
