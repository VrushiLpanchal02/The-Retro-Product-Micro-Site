San Francisco, CA December 15, 1954
"Technology That Actually Works!"

As an electrical engineer, I'm fascinated by the transistor technology, but what really matters is how it performs - and it performs brilliantly. The sound quality is impressive for such a small speaker, and the tuning is precise and stable. I've compared it side-by-side with my expensive console radio, and while the TR-1 can't match the bass response, the overall clarity and detail is surprisingly good. For the price and size, this is an engineering marvel. Highly recommended!

"Great Radio, Minor Tuning Issues"

I love my TR-1! It's so convenient to have in the bedroom without all the cords. The sound is clear and loud enough for my needs. My only complaint is that the tuning can be a bit touchy sometimes - you have to be very precise with the dial to get the best reception. Once you find a station, it stays tuned well, but finding that sweet spot takes some practice. Overall, I'm very happy with this purchase and would recommend it to friends.

Thomas Anderson

Chicago, IL December 8, 1954
"Good Concept, Could Use Improvement"

The TR-1 is definitely innovative and works as advertised. I appreciate having a portable radio, and the sound quality is decent. However, I expected a bit more volume for a $40 radio. It's fine for personal listening but not loud enough for group settings. Also, the tuning knob is quite small and can be hard to grip. These are minor issues, but worth mentioning. I think with a few tweaks, this could be an exceptional product.
