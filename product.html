Meet the Regency TR-1

The world’s first pocket transistor radio, styled for 1954 and ready for your pocket.

Dark Brown Finish

Specs That Change Everything:

  • 4 germanium transistors for portable power.
  • Superheterodyne AM receiver.
  • 3 × 5 × 1.25 inches, just 12 ounces with battery.
  • Over 20 hours of listening on a single 22.5V battery.
