Quick Help Tips

  • If reception is weak, fully extend the antenna and rotate the radio slowly to find the strongest signal.
  • If sound fades, replace the 22.5V battery and make sure the contacts are clean.
  • Keep the radio away from heavy metal objects and thick walls when listening indoors.
